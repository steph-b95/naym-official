ABOUT NAYM

NAYM is a young talented artist, who experiments with different genres and styles. Since 2020 he has struggled to find his direction of musicmaking, but finally has found his own sound. It reaches from Pop to Indie and even Rock. With his music, he is trying to help people out, by writing relatable lyrics. He wants to make the songs he needed in his bad times, so now when he does, others don't feel misunderstood or alone, when going through a Down in their life. When listening to his music, he wants the listeners to dive in a dreamworld by just letting the music work its magic on them. A quick journey into another reality.
